About the Project

The AI-powered Movie Review Detector is an advanced system that uses machine learning and natural language processing to identify potentially fake movie reviews.

Our system analyzes various aspects of a review, including linguistic patterns, sentiment analysis, and contextual relevance to determine the authenticity of movie reviews. This helps maintain the integrity of review platforms and assists users in making informed decisions.
